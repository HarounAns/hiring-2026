## Recommended Questions (Pick 3-4)

1. **Open:** "Walk me through the Revin AI agent that books appointments. How does it actually work — how does it decide what to say, when to book, and how does it integrate into ServiceTitan?"
   - You understand this domain. If his explanation is shallow, you'll know.

2. **Soundry exit:** "You left your own YC company. Why?"
   - The answer matters less than how he answers it.

3. **Owner test:** "At Revin, you work with 20+ companies. Give me an example where a client wanted something and you told them no, or redirected them to something different."
   - Owner vs order-taker. Maps directly to Phase 3 of the technical interview.

4. **Gut check:** "If I dropped you into our codebase tomorrow — 40 dealerships live, AI doing outbound SMS, scaling to 200 — what's your first week?"
