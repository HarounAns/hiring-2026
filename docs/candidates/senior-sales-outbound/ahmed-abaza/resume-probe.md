## Recommended Questions (Pick 3-4)

1. **Open:** "Tell me about the AI agent you built at AWS — the one piloted with 30 teams. Whose idea was it? How much of the product direction was yours?"
   - The distinction between "I designed this" and "I was assigned to this project" is everything.

2. **Side project:** "You built a rewards engine on Supabase. Why? Is it live? Do people use it?"
   - If he built it because he saw a real problem and wanted to solve it — that's the 0-to-1 instinct you need. If it's a learning exercise, noted.

3. **Owner instinct test:** "You're running the security agent project at AWS and someone on another team tells you the approach is wrong — that you should use a different model or a different architecture. What do you do?"
   - Does he defend his decisions with reasoning, or defer to authority? Owner vs executor.

4. **Gut check:** "If I dropped you into our codebase tomorrow — 40 dealerships live, AI doing outbound SMS, scaling to 200 — what's your first week?"
