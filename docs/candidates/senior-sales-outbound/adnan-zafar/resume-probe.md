## 1. Set the Frame

Open with the role change. Be direct — he needs to understand what he's signing up for before you probe anything.

- "Hey Adnan — quick update before we dive in. We filled the LLM reliability role you were interviewing for. But we have another position I think could be a fit — and it's a bigger opportunity."
- "It's our outbound sales product. AI reaches out to dealership leads via SMS, has conversations, books appointments. 40 dealers live today, scaling to 200."
- "The difference: this is a 0→1 role. You'd own the entire product end-to-end. No PM, no spec. You decide what to build, you talk to the dealers, you ship it."
- "I want to use this call to figure out together if this is the right fit for you. It's a different animal than reliability — so be honest with me about your reaction."

**Listen for:** Does he lean in or pull back? Does he ask questions about the product, or questions about the team/support structure? The first instinct matters.
